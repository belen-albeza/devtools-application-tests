Multiple Service Workers

  1. Open the Application panel and go to the Service Workers page.
  2. Click on this button
  3. A service worker registered under service-workers/multiple-workers/ must appear.
  4. Click on this button
  5. Another service worker must appear with the status Installed
  6. Click on the Unregister button in the Application panel to clean up

Note: after some time in step #4, it's normal that one of the workers disappear, and only one remains.
